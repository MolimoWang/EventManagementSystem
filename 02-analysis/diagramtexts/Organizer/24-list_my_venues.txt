title Organizer List My Venues  Page


actor Organizer
boundary VenueSystem
control VenueService

note right of Organizer:1. The organizer selects the "My Venues" \noption from the main page.
Organizer ->> VenueSystem : listMyEvents(List<Events>)
activate VenueSystem

note right of VenueSystem:2. The system turn my venues page(25-list_my_venues.png) \nto the displays a list of all the venues created.
loop For every Venue
 VenueSystem ->> VenueService : getVenue(Venue)
  activate VenueService
   VenueService -->> VenueSystem :return Venue
   deactivate VenueService
end

VenueSystem -->> Organizer :return my event messages
deactivate VenueSystem
