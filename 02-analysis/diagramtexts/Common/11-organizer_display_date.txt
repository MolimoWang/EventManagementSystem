title Organizer Display Date


actor "Organizer" as a
control ": EventSystem" as s
participant ": EventService" as u
entity "all : Event" as m
entity "dateEvents : Event" as sm

==1. The organizer is on their main page **(17-view_all_events.png)** or list my events page **(18-list_my_events.png)**.==

note right of a:The organizer enters the date they want to view.
a->s:DisplayDate(date)
activate s

s->u: getEvents(date)
activate u

loop [for every Event]
u -> m: getDate()
end loop
u-->s:return dateEvents
deactivate u

s->s:displayEvents(dateEvents)

s-->a:
deactivate s

==The selected date and all events scheduled for that date will be displayed on the screen.==
