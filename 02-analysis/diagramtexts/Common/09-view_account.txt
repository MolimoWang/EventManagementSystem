participant AccountSystem
participant AccountService
participant Account

 AccountService->>Account: getAccountName(name)

 end
  Account-->AccountService:return choosed Account details
 AccountService-->AccountSystem:return choosed Account details



 deactivate Account


deactivate AccountService
AccountSystem->AccountSystem:return choosed Account Details page
AccountSystem-->Organizer:
