@startuml
title View venue usage history

actor "Admin" as a
control ": EventSystem" as s
participant ": EventService" as u
entity "all : Event" as ae

note right of a: 1.The organizer wants to see venue usage history

a->s:getVenueUsage(venueName)
activate s

note right of s: 2.The system passes requests to the service.
s->u: getVenueUsage(venueName)
activate u
activate ae

note right of u: 3.The service queries this venue usage history among all events.
loop for every event
  u->ae:getUsage(venueName)
  ae -->u: return targetEvent
end

deactivate ae

u-->s:return targetEvents
deactivate u

note right of s: 4.The system displays the venue usage history to the organizer .
s->s:displayVenueUsage(targetEvents)
s-->a:
deactivate s

@enduml
